Welcome to My World Music

Hello! My name is Joaquin, and I’m a pianist born and raised in Peru. I am currently studying at Berklee College of Music in Boston, where I’m pursuing a double degree in Film Scoring and Performance. My musical journey is a blend of my roots, my influences, and my curiosity for sound, rhythm, and storytelling through music.

My trio explores different styles, combining the improvisational spirit of jazz with the soul of Afro-Peruvian rhythms, funk grooves, and the emotion of soul and hip-hop. We aim to create a sound that feels both modern and deeply connected to our heritage.
